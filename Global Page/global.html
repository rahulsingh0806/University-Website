Center for Global Education

Academic Mentorship from some of the best academic institutions from Canada and Australia is Amriti University’s initiative to bring the world’s best education experience for Indian students, to their doorstep.

We have launched full-time pathway degree programs in Academic Mentorship with some leading colleges and universities of the world so that our students get access to globally relevant curriculum, and exposure to accomplished international faculty. Our students will study an applied curriculum that is taught anywhere in the world with an option to transfer to our International partner institutions with 100% credit transfers. You will almost pay 1/3 of the international tuition fees for a similar curriculum when you start at Amriti University and an option to seamlessly transfer (based on conditions) in the third year of an undergraduate degree in Canada or Australia. Welcome to an opportunity that will change how you study and succeed in a connected world.
